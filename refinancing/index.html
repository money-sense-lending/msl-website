We're here to help you Refinance your Home Loan or Investment Loan

If your home loan has been with the same bank for two or more years, there’s a good chance you’re paying more than you need to. We’re a Traralgon-based broker helping homeowners across the Latrobe Valley and Gippsland check their rate against the whole market — 30+ lenders — and switch when it genuinely pays off.

Our refinance review is honest — sometimes the answer is “stay put”

About one in five refinances we review don’t save enough to be worth the paperwork, and we’ll tell you that straight. For the rest, the maths is compelling: a sharper rate on a typical Valley mortgage commonly saves hundreds per month, and some lenders offer cashback incentives on top. We calculate your break-even point — switching costs versus monthly savings — before you commit to anything.

Reasons Gippsland homeowners refinance with us

  • Rate check — your loyalty tax is real; banks save their best rates for new customers. Start with our 60-second loan health check.
  • Debt consolidation — roll high-interest cards and personal loans into one repayment.
  • Equity release — fund a renovation, a deposit on an investment property, or a build without touching savings.
  • Fixed rate ending — coming off a fixed term is the single best moment to compare the whole market. Our fixed rate expiry tool can track it for you.
  • Restructure — wrong loan type, wrong split, or cross-collateralised properties quietly costing you flexibility.

Refinancing a Loan with Money Sense Lending

Initial Refinance Consultation

A Finance & Mortgage Broker will take the time to understand your current loan situation and discuss refinancing options available from banks and lenders across Australia. We'll guide you through the next steps of the refinancing application process, ensuring you have access Home Loan options and the most competitive refinance interest rates.

Fact Find

We'll work with you to understand a bit more about your situation (including your financials, assets etc.) to make sure you will qualify for refinancing. We will also check eligibility for special lender policies that might benefit you.

Pre Approval

Once you receive pre-approval for the refinancing, you can move forward with confidence. We will outline the new loan amount, interest rates (both variable and fixed), and any potential interest rate discounts. Whether you are looking to reduce loan repayments, consolidate debts, or change your Home Loan term, we ensure you access loan options from banks and lenders nationwide.

Settlement

During the settlement process, your existing loan will be paid off, and your new loan will be drawn down. We will assist you in understanding all associated costs, including any fees or charges related to the refinancing.

Refinancing questions, answered

How much could refinancing actually save me?

As a rough guide, trimming 0.5% off a $450,000 loan saves about $140 a month — over $40,000 across the life of the loan. Run your own numbers in our free Loan Health Check, or call and we'll do it with you.

Does refinancing hurt my credit score?

One application has minimal impact. What hurts is applying to lots of lenders separately. A broker compares 30+ lenders first and applies once, to the right one — that's the smart order.

What does it cost to switch lenders?

Usually a few hundred dollars in discharge and government fees. Between sharper rates and lender cashback offers, most people are ahead within months — and we always do that maths with you before anything moves.

Are cashback offers worth chasing?

Sometimes — but a slightly lower rate usually beats a headline cashback over the life of the loan. We compare both ways and show you the true winner, not the shiniest ad.
